MedRCM Timesheet Application Password Management Guide

This guide explains how to manage your password in the MedRCM Timesheet Application, including how to change your password after logging in and how to reset your password if you've forgotten it.

Changing Your Password After Login

Step 1: Access the User Menu

After logging into the MedRCM Timesheet Application, you will see your username (e.g., "medrcm.admin03") in the top-right corner of the navigation bar. Click on your username to open the dropdown menu.

Image 1: The Employee Dashboard showing the main interface of the timesheet application.

Step 2: Select "Change Password"

From the dropdown menu, click on the "Change Password" option. This will take you to the password change page.

Image 2: The dropdown menu displaying the "Change Password" option when clicking on the username.

Step 3: Enter Password Information

On the Change Password page, you'll need to complete three fields:

  1. Current Password: Enter your existing password (the placeholder indicates "demo/admin" as an example)
  2. New Password: Create your new password
  3. Confirm New Password: Re-enter your new password to verify it matches
Image 3: The Change Password form with fields for current password, new password, and confirmation.

Step 4: Update Your Password

After filling in all three fields, click the "Update Password" button to save your changes. The system will process your request and update your password.

Resetting Your Forgotten Password

Step 1: Access the Login Page

Navigate to the login page of the MedRCM Timesheet Application.

Image 4: The login page with username and password fields.

Step 2: Click "Forgot Password?"

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link located below the login button.

Step 3: Enter Your Employee ID

On the Reset Password page, you'll be prompted to enter your Employee ID to verify your account.

Image 5: The Reset Password page requesting employee ID verification.

Step 4: Verify Your Identity

After entering your Employee ID, click the "Verify" button to proceed.

Step 5: Create New Password

After verification, you'll be taken to a page where you can set a new password:

  1. New Password: Enter your desired new password
  2. Confirm New Password: Re-enter the same password to confirm
Image 6: The password reset page with empty fields for entering a new password.
Image 7: The password reset page showing the completed password fields with instructions.

Step 6: Complete Password Reset

Click the "Reset Password" button after entering and confirming your new password. Once processed, your password will be reset, and you can use the new password to log in.

Important Notes

  • Always ensure your passwords are secure by using a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters
  • Remember to keep your password confidential
  • The system may have specific password requirements (minimum length, character types, etc.)
  • If you continue to experience issues, contact your system administrator
